Senior Digital Designer

London, hybrid, with flexible hours

We’re looking for a Senior Digital Designer who thrives in a collaborative, forward-thinking environment to join our team. You will help us shape the future of our clients’ brands through cutting-edge digital experiences, bringing bold ideas to life with empathy and imagination.

About Output:

We are defiant optimists who partner with clients to create brands and digital experiences that inspire meaningful change. Our independent, female-led design studio thrives on innovation and collaboration, building distinctive brands with purpose and character. We’re passionate about making a positive impact, and our clients share this vision, seeking to stand out and shake things up in their industries.

Key responsibilities:

  • Collaborate closely with the brand and strategy teams to translate characterful brands into impactful digital experiences across web, apps, and more.
  • Create intuitive user interfaces with a strong focus on user experience (UX), information architecture, and visual storytelling.
  • Lead digital design projects from concept through delivery.
  • Produce excellent creative, and nail the strategy.
  • Explore new ways to challenge the norms of digital design.
  • Present and communicate ideas confidently to clients.
  • Work with our dev partners to ensure your final product functions as well as it looks.

 

Skills & experience:

  • 5+ years of experience in digital design, preferably within a creative or product agency.
  • A strong portfolio. Show us robust systems thinking combined with memorable creative. We want to see characterful experiences, and evidence of seeing a brand concept though components.
  • Expertise in Figma is a must. You’re a whizz prototyping in Figma. You think about how things move and work.
  • Solid understanding of information architecture, wireframes, responsive design principles, and front-end development processes.
  • Experience working with developers to deliver seamless design-to-code handover.
  • The ability to articulate and present ideas clearly.
  • A passion for making the way we do all of this even better – whether its an interest in emerging digital trends, running retros, or thinking about how to better collaborate and co-create.

 

What we offer:

  • A creative and collaborative studio environment where your ideas are encouraged and valued.
  • Flexible working options, including a 9-day fortnight and work-from-anywhere opportunities.
  • Personalised career development plans and monthly check-ins to support your professional growth.
  • Benefits such as Vitality healthcare, extended paid leave during the Christmas shutdown, and team offsite events.
  • The opportunity to work on purposeful projects for clients who are making a positive difference in the world.

 

How to apply:
Send your CV, portfolio, and a brief note on why you’re a great fit for this role to johanna@studio-output.com.
